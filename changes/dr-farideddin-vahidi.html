Specialist Pediatrics

Dr. Farideddin Vahidi is an accomplished Specialist Pediatrician with over 25 years of experience in the pediatric field including 14 years of dedicated service in Dubai. Proficient in effectively handling a diverse range of infant and child illnesses across emergency departments, pediatric wards, and ambulatory clinics.

My daily responsibilities revolve around assessing, admitting, and managing acutely ill patients in the emergency room or outpatient clinics, where I meticulously design personalized care plan and provide individualized follow-up for each patient. In the pediatric ward, my approach to patient admissions fosters a collaborative, multidisciplinary teamwork ethos aimed at elevating the overall quality of care.
