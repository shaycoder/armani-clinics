Dr. Haseena Fuad

  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (1996 - 2002)
  • Master of Dental Surgery, Periodontics (2007 - 2010)

Biological dentist & Specialist Periodontist

Dr. Haseena Fuad is an expert in treating periodontal disease in patients with pre-existing conditions like diabetes, hypertension, pregnancy and hormone-associated gingival conditions in post menopausal women. She also does many aesthetic plastic gingival corrections for gummy smiles and depigmentation of dark gums.

Dr Haseena treats both Kids and Adults.

Outside work she is a motivational speaker, voice- over expert and loves driving.

Specialized in

  • Surgical and nonsurgical periodontal procedures
  • Dental Implants (Nobel Biocare, Corticobasal implants)
  • Periodontal regenerative surgeries with PRF
  • Gingival depigmentation
  • Vestibuloplasty
  • Gingivectomy
  • Gingivoplasty
  • Periodontal microsurgery
  • Ozone therapy
  • Open flap debridement
  • Free gingival graft
  • Connective tissue graft
  • Guided Bone Regeneration
  • Guided tissue regeneration
  • Hemisection and root resection
